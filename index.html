Algebra Penguins

Guidelines

You have 60 seconds to complete as many equations as you can. There are two difficulty levels, easy and normal. You get 1 point for correctly answering an easy question and 2 points for a normal equation. You can change between the difficulty levels during the 60 seconds, just click on the corresponding button and it will display that difficulty repeatedly until changed.
